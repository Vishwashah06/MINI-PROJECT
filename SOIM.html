SILVER OAK UNIVERSITY OF MANAGEMENT(SOIM)

Silver Oak Institute of Management(SOIM) was established in the year 2019. With the University’s expansion and steady growth, department of management aims to prepare students for corporate life through contemporary academic rigor, which shall build their competence in various management discipline. Silver Oak University (SOU) has made an attempt to look forward for bridging the gap between the academic degree and the corporate industry requirements and enable students to achieve excellence in every aspect of life. pedagogy and andragogy approaches for imparting the curriculum to the students through Summer Internship Programme(SIP) Comprehensive cape-stone projects to provide hands-on experience Industrial visits to eminent companies at regular intervals Celebrated guest speakers are invited to deliver expert talks Management Conclaves Case Study Analysis News Paper Discussion Forums Trans Disciplinary Learning. Logic & Critical Thinking TED Talks SOU believes in the philosophy of excellence, wherein “Quality is not an Act, it is a habit’’. The excellence is only achieved when faculty members and students share healthy relationship, which makes the class room sessions not only interactive but increases the receptivity of students. SOIM has experienced and expert faculty members which drawn from a variety of functional management disciplines. We believe that an institute can excel when the faculty members and the students share a healthy relationship. It not only helps make the class interactive but also enhances the level of receptivity of the students. To maintain it, experienced and expert faculty members are drawn from a variety of functional areas of management. The growth of faculty members is much required as students; thus, faculties are encouraged to engage in research, consulting, and sponsored research. Faculty development programmes are organized to keep them flexible in responding to educational queries of the students. Silver Oak Institute of Management, Silver Oak University believes in student and faculty empowerment to achieve the desired goals.
